Participate in a Research Study

This HIT is part of a research study conducted by researchers at the University of Washington. To complete this HIT, follow the steps below:

  1. Determine if you are eligible. To participate you must:

    • Have not taken this study before.
    • Be at least 18 years of age.
    • Be free from red-green or total color blindness.
    • Use a browser with javascript enabled.

    If you are eligble, complete all the following steps in order to submit. The "Complete Task" button will submit the HIT, but will not be active unless all steps have been completed. Checkmarks should indicate progress at each task.
