Perception of Correlation

Sometimes, two different variables are related. That is, a change in one is connected with a change in the other. For instance, there is a relationship between the seasons and the temperature: it is colder in the winter, and warmer in the summer.

Regression analysis is a field of statistics that tries to find and describe these relationships. For instance, how a variable X (the dependent variable) is related to another variable Y (the independent variable). For this task, you will be asked to visually estimate the relationships between variables in a scatterplot. In general, how does Y (the vertical position of points) change with X (the horizontal position of points).

Not all relationships are equally strong. For this task, we are asking you to estimate the general trend. This may be difficult if the relationship is weak.
